1.4 Transcription

Procedure

  1. Initiation

    • start point: TATA box
    • transcription factors: TFIID, TFIIB (具有核酸结合能力的特殊蛋白质)
    • transcription initiation complex: 转录起始复合物
    • 研究方法：荧光报告基因，二代测序

  2. Elongation

    • RNA polymerase II: untwists the double helix. Transcribed simultaneously by several polymerases.
    • Nucleotides are added to the 3’ end of the growing RNA molecule
      • DNA转录的方向性: 3’–>5’
    • Speed: 40 nucleotides/s in eukaryotes
    • The new RNA molecule peels away from its DNA template
    • The DNA double helix reforms.

  3. Termination

    • Different in bacteria and eukaryotes

      • Bacteria: polymerase stops transcription at the terminator, and the mRNA can be translated without further modification;
      • Eukaryotes: RNA polymerase II transcribes the polyadenylation signal sequence, and the transcription is released 10–35 nucleotides past the signal sequence.

    • Eukaryote: pre-mRNA molecule. The 5’ end receives a modified nucleotide cap; the 3’ end gets a poly-A tail.

      • Function: 3’ UTR region facilitates the export of mNRA to the cytoplasm; 3’ poly-A protects mRNA from hydrolytic enzymes; 5’ cap help ribosomes attach to the 5’ end.

  4. (Post) RNA splicing: removes introns (non-coding) and joins exons, creating an mRNA molecule with a continuous coding sequence

    • alternative splicing 可变性剪切: exon skipping, intron retention ,alternative 5’ splice site, alternative 3’ splice site, mutually exclusive exons

      • Contains 60–95 nt (nucleotide), commonly 76

      • It acts as an adapter molecule for linking amino acids to its specific codon in the mRNA template.

      • Diagram of the tRNA

      • Wobbling: 不完全遵循碱基互补配对

    • Ribosome: rRNAs and r-proteins，复杂的蛋白质复合体

      • Components: S—沉降系数，离心区分

      • Generation: ribosome components are transcribed by Pol I, Pol II, and Pol III： 不同RNA聚合酶 (Pol) 的功能和对应产物

        • The synthesis of mRNA is a complex process in eukaryotes. Compared to prokaryotes, where only a single polymerase is required to bind with the DNA strands, eukaryotes require three different polymerases, along with an additional set of transcriptional factors (TFs) for transcription and translation. The three polymerases required are RNA polymerase I, II, and III.

      • Structure: 2009 Nobel Prize. Several active centers: mRNA binding, E site, P site, G factor site, 5S site, polypeptide exit site

        • Ribosomes appear flattened and spherical when viewed under an electron microscope, with a diameter ranging between 15 to 25 nm.

        • These structures are comprised of two major ribonucleoprotein subunits. The two subunits assemble to translate mRNA and disassemble when translation is complete.

        • The simplest ribosome is from bacteria and comprises the 30S and 50S subunit, forming the 70S ribosome. In contrast, yeast ribosomes contain the 40S and 60S subunits, which assemble to form the 80S structure.

        • Each subunit across species contains three sites that underpin the translation process. These are designated the A (aminoacyl), which accommodates the incoming tRNA which is primed with an amino acid (aminoacylated-tRNA); P (peptidyl), which holds the tRNA with the emerging, or nascent, peptide chain (peptidyl-tRNA); and E (exit), which mobilizes the emerging tRNA which has been stripped of its amino acid (deacylated-tRNA).

      • Distribution

        • Protein complex: A protein complex or multiprotein complex is a group of two or more associated polypeptide chains. Protein complexes are distinct from multienzyme complexes, in which multiple catalytic domains are found in a single polypeptide chain.

3.4 Modification

  • Irreversible modification: 多为切割

    • Removal of N-Met (N端甲硫氨酸)
    • Proteolytic processing (蛋白酶切割)
      • e.g., blood coagulation
      • Caspase 胱天蛋白酶: serve as a primary effector during apoptosis to proteolytically dismantle most cellular structures

  • reversible modification: chemical

    • Phosphorylation 磷酸化
      • kinase 激酶：水解ATP，介导磷酸化。能够催化从高能供体分子转移磷酸基团到特定靶分子（底物）的酶
      • 一般而言，磷酸化的目的是“激活”或“能化”底物，增大它的能量，以使其可参加随后的自由能负变化的反应。
    • Acetylation 乙酰化
      • 组蛋白的乙酰化修饰与表观遗传学（epigenetics）
      • 组织蛋白（histone，组蛋白）是真核生物体细胞染色质与原核细胞中的碱性蛋白质，和DNA共同组成核小体结构
    • Methylation 甲基化
    • Ubiquitination 泛素化
    • Glycosylation 糖基化
      • N-糖基化、O-糖基化：是蛋白质成熟的重要标志
      • 功能：帮助正确折叠、防止降解（against proteolysis）、形成胞外基质（ECM）、调制免疫反应
    • Myristylation 十四烷基化
    • Farnesylation 法尼基化

  • Fluorescence Resonace Energy Trasfer (FRET) 荧光共振能量转移

  • Biacore (Surface Plasmon Resonance, SPR) 表面等离子共振

    • 玻璃+金属膜，全反射入射时的倏逝波引发金属中的自由电子产生表面等离子体，当表面等离子体与倏逝波频率相等时，将发生共振吸收，全反射光急剧减弱
    • 共振角的改变与所结合的分子质量改变有关

4.3 Genetics and diseases

Genetic disorders

  • Monogenetic disorder 单基因

    • Expressivity 表现度
    • Penetrance 外显率
    • Pleotropic 多效性
    • Heterogeneity 异质性

  • Polygenetic disorder

    • Normal distribution of allele frequency
    • Phenotype transfer function: phenotype multifactorial inhertance depends on transfer function
    • “regression of average ”: 极端表型趋近于平均值

  • GWAS (Genome–Wide Association Study)

    • Association study 关联性研究：to identify genetic risk factor

  • Mitochondrial genes

    • prone to mutation (no histone protection, etc.)
    • Mitochondrial disorder –> energy –> neurosystem
    • e.g. MELAS disease

  • Genetic imprinting and phenotype

    • Gene imprinting: 基因的蛋白质修饰（甲基化）
    • DNA methylation to repress gene expression
    • Maternal imprinting, Paternal imprinting
    • imprinted genes play an important role in development
    • e.g. Angelman syndrome
    • assisted reproductive technology (ART) associated with greater risk of imprinting disorders: ART is performed during the critical periods of epigenetic reprogramming

  • Cellular genetic variation

  • Genetic approaches to treat diseases

    • classification

    • strategy

      • In-vivo; ex-vivo

    • delivery

    • e.g., Regeneration of the entire human epidermis using transgenic stem cells [Nature 2017]

    • e.g., Chimeric antigen receptor T cells (CAR-T therapy)
