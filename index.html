Illumination strategy in Fourier ptychography

2022-11-07
Notes / Optics

This is a mini-review focused on the illumination strategy of Fourier ptychography. It is a course project in academic writing (credit: Prof. Pan Wang, Prof. Dawei Di). It is worth mentioning that the derived SBP-T is a valuable and effective classification metric of microscopy techniques.

I have submitted it on arxiv. By the way, it took me almost the entire week to finish reading and writing. Thanks to our teammates, especially Haibo Xu, for the valuable discussion of the SBP-T derivation. Through this work, I better understand the numerical aperture (NA) of the optical system, though it is still slightly elusive.

I would also like to thank Starbucks for supporting me with the supportive coffee :)
